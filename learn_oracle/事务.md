## 事务

- commit，ROLLBACK

  ```plsql
  --COMMIT/ROLLBACK
  UPDATE,INSERT,DELETE
  COMMIT;--将事务持久化,并将所有的锁释放
  ```

- SAVEPOINT

  ```plsql
  --设置断点
  statements;
  savepoint POINT1;
  statements;
  savepoint POINT2;
  statements;
  savepoint POINT3;
  ROLLBACK TO POINT2;  --POINT2与POINT3之间的操作消失,POINT2之前的事务仍保存在缓存中,仍需commit
  ```
